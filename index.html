Game Features

Exciting gameplay mechanic

Your adventure begins with a tutorial where you select a character to guide you through the basics. After that, you choose a mentor from the Straw Hat Pirates to specialize in your chosen profession. For example, Zoro will teach you the art of swordsmanship, Nami will guide you through navigation and map-related tasks, and Sanji will share his expertise in close combat and cooking.

Immersive storyline

This story is that as the ultimate treasure of the One Piece world has not been found, the Celestial Dragons' control over the world has become stronger, and our game characters have been violated, so they are inspired to overthrow their rule and protect innocent villagers.

Remastered animated action scenes

We use the game to remake some same senses in the animation to let players have an immersive experience.

Multiplayer mode

Join other online players in the hunt for rare resources and treasures. we add dynamic and social elements to the game, ensuring there is always something new and exciting to do.

Customizable characters

Unlock various styles through tasks, exploration, and purchases. you can equip special clothes to boost strength. Customize your character's appearance and pirate ship, and you can get free paintings and decals from the player community.
